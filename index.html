Kerala is renowned for some of its unique cultural and geographical characteristics. The practices and traditions handed down from generation to generation, alongside some of the natural wonders that we have been blessed with have attracted people to this land for centuries. Coming to Kerala, you will be greeted with breathtaking natural wonders; vibrant wildlife; picturesque hill stations; delicious veg, non-veg delicacies; and spectacular dance forms, music and handicraft. National Geographic Traveler has listed Kerala among the 10 paradises of the world.
